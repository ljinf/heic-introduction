## Made a free tool that converts HEIC to PNG/JPG entirely in your browser – no uploads, no privacy worries

If you shoot photos in HEIC on your iPhone (like I do), you've probably run into the same annoyance: trying to send a photo to someone on Windows, or upload it to a website that doesn't support HEIC, and getting that dreaded "format not supported" message.

I got tired of hunting for converters that either:

- Charge money for basic features

- Force you to upload your photos to some random server (privacy nightmare)

- Have terrible UX with ads everywhere

So I built a free tool that solves all of this: HEICApp.com

What it does:

- Converts HEIC/HEIF to PNG or JPG

- Batch conversion – select multiple files at once

- All processing happens locally in your browser – zero uploads, zero server storage

- JPG quality slider (10%–100%)

- One-click ZIP download for all converted files

Why I built it:
I wanted something that just works, respects privacy, and doesn't cost anything. No sign-ups, no ads, no nonsense.

Tech stack: Vue 3 + heic2any + JSZip, all client-side.

Would love to hear your feedback or feature requests. If you find it useful, feel free to share with others who might be dealing with the same HEIC headache.

Link: https://www.heicapp.com

P.S. If you're on iPhone and your HEIC files aren't being recognized, make sure your camera is set to "High Efficiency" (Settings > Camera > Formats > High Efficiency).
